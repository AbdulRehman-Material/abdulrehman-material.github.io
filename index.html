I am a Material Engineer. I'm working at the intersection of materials & manufacturing processes, and industrial quality systems. My work involves materials inspection, controlling manufacturing process, and standards compliance for gas distribution systems, with a focus on material integrity, durability, safety, and process reliability. Alongside this, I have experience in mineral processing research, including ore characterization, hydrometallurgical extraction, and process optimization, as well as exposure to welding quality systems and ASME-based fabrication standards.

I hold a B.S. in Metallurgy and Materials Engineering from the University of the Punjab, with a strong academic foundation in materials characterization, processing, and manufacturing principles. I have developed technical proficiency in OriginPro and Python for data analysis, ANSYS for simulation (entry-level), and Fusion 360 and AutoCAD for modeling and design tasks, supported by practical engineering exposure. I was awarded 1st position in my final year poster presentation for research communication.

My research interests lie in advanced and functional materials, with a strong focus on modern manufacturing and processing technologies. I am particularly motivated by sustainable approaches to metal recovery and recycling, as well as the development of next-generation energy materials.
